WYMeditor is a browser based WYSIWYM editor written in JavaScript.

By separating content and presentation the editor is both easier to use than regular WYSIWYG editors and that makes sure you have full control of both your content and what your site looks like.

WYMeditor generates valid strict XHTML, is supported in all major browsers and can easily be integrated with any content management system. WYMeditor is actually already available on over 40 different platforms.
