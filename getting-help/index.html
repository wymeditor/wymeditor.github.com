Getting Help

There are several ways to get help with WYMeditor – here are a few.

The docs

This is a really good place to start. Our GitHub Wiki covers everything from getting started to advanced customization and plugin development.

The Forum

If you still cant figure things out the forum is full of helpful people, or at least we want it to be. Go and make your voice heard!

Freenode

Most of the time you will find someone lurking in #wymeditor on Freenode.net – please come in and talk with us!
